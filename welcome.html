03 / 06
Privacy

Built for privacy.
Not a feature — a rule.

Eclipse blocks ads and trackers at the network level before they load. Incognito mode uses a fully separate Electron session — no history, no cookies, no cache, ever.

Network-level adblock

Domains blocked in Electron's main process — nothing loads at all.

Real incognito

Separate Electron session partition. Closed = gone. No exceptions.

Zero telemetry

Eclipse sends nothing. No crash reports, no analytics, no pings.

Password Manager

Your passwords.
Encrypted. Local.

Eclipse includes a built-in AES-encrypted password vault protected by a master password. No cloud. No subscription. Per-profile isolation so your work logins never touch your personal ones.

AES encryption

Your vault is encrypted with a master password only you know.

Per-profile vaults

Each profile has an entirely separate encrypted password store.
