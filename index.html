Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Eclipse is built around a modular core. Every feature is intentional, every piece is replaceable.

Multi-Tab Engine

Full multi-tab browsing with per-tab isolation, fast switching, and session persistence across restarts.

Real Incognito Mode

A fully isolated Electron session. No history, no cookies, no cache — gone when you close the window.

Network-Level Adblock

File-based blocklist intercepted at the Electron main process. Blocks ads and trackers before they even load.

Multi-Profile System

Separate profiles with isolated bookmarks, history, settings, and extensions. Switch instantly from the toolbar.

Modular Extensions

Add only what you need. Notes, Todo, Translator, Dark Reader, and Performance Monitor — all sandboxed.

Local Password Manager

AES-encrypted, master-password-protected vault stored entirely on your machine. Zero cloud dependency.

Floating Mini-Apps

Draggable, resizable windows that float over your browsing. Dock them, move them, or picture-in-picture them.

20+ Themes

Live theme switching with custom accent colors, icon tints, and dark/light variants stored as JSON files.

Privacy

Built for privacy.
Designed for control.

Every decision in Eclipse was made with your data in mind — and it never leaves your machine.

Zero Telemetry

Eclipse sends nothing. No crash reports, no analytics, no usage data. Ever.

Ad & Tracker Blocking

Network-level interception blocks thousands of domains before a single byte loads.

100% Local Storage

Bookmarks, passwords, history, and profiles — all stored on your device, encrypted at rest.

Profile Isolation

Each profile is fully sandboxed. Nothing bleeds between work, personal, and incognito sessions.
